U Bo Htwe | Life Timeline

The Life of U Bo Htwe

From Kyauk Sin Village to a Life of Many Journeys

1970 — A Beginning

Born on July 23, 1970
(ဝါဆိုလဆုတ် ၅ ရက် ၁၃၃၂)

In the small fishing village of Kyauk Sin, a boy was born who would later become one of the few people from the village to graduate with a university degree.

Childhood

Growing up in Kyauk Sin, life revolved around the sea. Boats, fishing nets, and the rhythm of village life shaped his childhood.

Even from a young age, he was known for his intelligence and curiosity.

University Graduate

At a time when very few from the village pursued higher education, he worked hard, lived at a monastery, and followed his dream of becoming a graduate,eventually graduated with a university degree(Degree in History).

His achievement became a great pride for his family and the village.

A Man With Many Lives

Throughout his life, he worked in many roles:

  • Government Forest Office
  • Motorcycle Taxi Driver
  • Writer
  • Song Writer

Each chapter reflected resilience and adaptability through changing times.

Political Life

With a passion for society and the people, he later entered politics and served the community in another meaningful way.

Retirement

After decades of experience, hard work, and service, he is now enjoying retirement.

His story continues through the memories, wisdom, and legacy he leaves behind.
